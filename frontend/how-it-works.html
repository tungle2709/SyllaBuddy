How It Works

1. Upload Your Syllabus

Upload your syllabus in PDF format using the upload form on the homepage. The system supports most standard syllabus formats, including those with tables, bullet points, and detailed schedules. Simply drag and drop your file or select it from your device, and let the system handle the rest.

2. Auto-Schedule Tasks

Our AI-powered system scans your syllabus to extract deadlines, assignments, and task descriptions. It uses advanced natural language processing to identify key dates and organize them into a structured schedule. Whether it's quizzes, exams, or project deadlines, everything is automatically categorized for you.

The system also detects overlapping deadlines and provides suggestions to help you prioritize your tasks effectively.

3. View Upcoming Tasks

Visit the "Upcoming Tasks" page to see a real-time schedule of your assignments. Tasks are grouped by week and sorted by due date, making it easy to stay on top of your workload. Each task includes details such as the course name, description, due date, and weight, so you have all the information you need at a glance.

You can also mark tasks as completed directly on this page, helping you track your progress throughout the semester.

4. Stay Notified

Enable notifications to get reminders about upcoming tasks. You can customize the notification settings to receive alerts a day, an hour, or even 10 minutes before a deadline. Notifications are sent directly to your device, ensuring you never miss an important task.

Stay stress-free and focus on your studies while the system keeps you informed about what's next.

5. Track Your Progress

Mark tasks as completed to track your progress. The system provides a visual representation of your completed and pending tasks, helping you stay motivated. You can also view detailed statistics, such as the percentage of tasks completed for each course, to monitor your performance.

Tracking your progress ensures that you stay organized and achieve your academic goals efficiently.

6. Export Your Schedule

Download your schedule in various formats, such as PDF, Excel, or iCalendar, for offline access. You can also sync it with your favorite calendar app, including Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar, to integrate it seamlessly into your daily routine.

This feature ensures that your schedule is always accessible, whether you're on your laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

7. Collaborate with Peers

Share your schedule with classmates or group members to collaborate on assignments and projects. The system allows you to export and share specific tasks or entire schedules, making teamwork more efficient and organized.

Collaboration features ensure that everyone stays on the same page, reducing miscommunication and missed deadlines.

8. Get Insights and Recommendations

Our system provides insights into your workload, such as the busiest weeks or courses with the most assignments. It also offers recommendations to help you manage your time better, such as breaking large tasks into smaller, manageable chunks.

These insights empower you to plan ahead and maintain a healthy balance between academics and personal life.
